The Recovery

In the aftermath of the battle, Westeros begins the process of rebuilding. The alliances forged in the face of adversity persist, and the realm learns that strength lies not just in individual houses but in the unity of the living against the forces of darkness. The Wall, once a symbol of division, now stands as a testament to the resilience of those who defended it. The Night's Watch, revitalized and respected, continues its watch over the realms of men. The Free Folk, once considered enemies, find a new home and purpose within the realm.
