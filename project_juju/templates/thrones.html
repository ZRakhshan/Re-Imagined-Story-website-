Thrones of Shadows

Overview

Perched strategically atop a hill overlooking the capital city, Westeros Keep stands as a symbol of power and influence in the realm. Constructed with towering spires, formidable walls, and adorned with intricate carvings, the palace is a testament to the wealth and prestige of its inhabitants. The ruling house, whose banner flies high above the keep, governs the surrounding lands and plays a pivotal role in the intricate political landscape of Westeros.
